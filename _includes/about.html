Tech Community Support

Most women learning to code feel at least a little supported by the tech community. 31.3% of women learning to code feel 'Quite Supported', and 15.2% feel 'Very Supported'.

Low OpenSource Participation

There is a dearth of opensource participation among women learning to code. This one really hit home for me, as it was my opensource contributions that set me apart during my job search. To help, I've created a tutorial for getting started with the DEVcommunity opensource project.

Low Conference Attendance

Women learning to code are mederately under-represented at tech conferences. Almost all who have attended a conference went to an im-person one (as opposed to virtual).
